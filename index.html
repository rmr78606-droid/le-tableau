Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

At Le Tableau, we are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Egyptian Personal Data Protection Law No. 151 of 2020 and applicable regulations.

What data do we collect?

We collect your name, phone number, address, and email (optional) solely to process your orders and communicate with you. We do not store credit card details – all card payments are securely processed via Paymob.

How do we use your data?

• Order fulfilment and delivery.
• Sending promotional offers (you may unsubscribe at any time).
• Improving our services.
• Complying with legal obligations.

Your rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at privacy@letableau.com. We retain your data for 3 years for tax purposes as per Egyptian e‑commerce law.

Refund & Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Our return policy is governed by the Egyptian Consumer Protection Law No. 181 of 2018.

Return period

• 14 days from the date of delivery.
• Items must be unused and in original condition.
• Custom‑framed items are non‑returnable unless they have a manufacturing defect.

Return process

1. Contact us at returns@letableau.com within 14 days.
2. Attach a photo of the item and your order number.
3. We will arrange for a courier to pick up the item (shipping cost covered by us if defective).
4. After inspection, we will issue a refund within 7 business days to your original payment method.

Exceptions

• Items damaged due to misuse.
• Orders discounted with promo codes (only the paid amount is refunded).
• Items returned after 14 days.

Shipping Policy

Le Tableau partners with leading local couriers (Egypt Post, Aramex, and Shippify) to deliver your artwork swiftly and safely.

Delivery times

• Cairo & Giza: 2–3 business days
• Alexandria & Delta: 3–4 business days
• Other governorates: 4–6 business days
• Free shipping on orders above 1500 EGP. A flat fee of 40 EGP applies to orders below that amount.

Order tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an SMS with a tracking number. You can follow your shipment on the courier’s website.

Delayed delivery

If your order exceeds the estimated delivery time, please contact us at shipping@letableau.com and we will follow up immediately.
